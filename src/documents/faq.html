Got questions? We’ve got answers!

These FAQs are designed to provide a better understanding of FreightForge, our offering and our technical tools.

About FreightForge

What is FreightForge ?

Freightforge is a simple, fast and powerful spot quote and transport order application enabling shippers to find the right transport company for any shipment and helping carriers find more leads and business partners.

Who can use FreightForge ?

FreightForge is a business-to-business application connecting transport buyers with transport service providers. Any company can use FreightForge after registration.

Who can join FreightForge?

FreightForge has no geographic limitation. Any company can use the services regardless of its location. Currently, most of our shippers and carriers come from Europe and North America, and the majority of the transports orders are allocated to carriers having local activities. FreightForge is committed to support geographic expansion. (If you think you are one of the first company in your geography, contact us and we can talk about a mutually beneficial deal to expand in your region).

What goods can I freight with FreightForge ?

FreightForge can deal with any goods that can be legally transported.

What is the geographical coverage of FreightForge ?

FreightForge has no geographic boundaries. The service is available globally.

What are the modalities available with FreightForge ?

FreightForge is modality-neutral; it offers access to road, air, rail, barge, deep and short sea carriers transporting goods all over the world.

Is it a freight exchange?

No. FreightForge offers a service that connects shippers and carriers. FreightForge goes beyond a simple freight exchange. It is driven by requests for quote sent by shippers. The requests for quote are sent to carriers whose profile matches the load. Carriers which receive a request for quote can quote on the transport.

Is FreightForge a reverse auction site?

No. FreightForge only ask the carrier to quote once. The carrier is always free to quote or not.

Can I have a shipper and a carrier account at the same time?

Yes. If you are a carrier interested in outsourcing transport, we encourage you to set up a shipper account, too.

How does FreightForge select the carriers to which the requests for quote are sent to?

At registration carriers are asked some question about their services capabilities and geographic coverage. FreightForge uses this information when matching a shipment with carriers.

Do I have to respond to a request for quote?

No. There is no obligation whatsoever. If – for any reason – your company is not interested in sending a quote for a specific load, simply disregard the invitation to quote without any negative consequences.

Do carriers see each other’s quotes?

No. Only the shipper which sent the request for quote can see the incoming quotes for that specific quote request.

Who selects the carrier that gets the job?

The shipper selects the carrier that wins the transport order. FreightForge remains neutral and never advises for or against a carrier.

How many users can I create for my account?

Unlimited number of users can be linked to one company account; FreightForge does not charge any extra fee for it.

How does Freightforge make money?

FreightForge earns money by taking a small percentage of the price of transport quoted and of the business won.

Getting started

What are the technical requirements of using FreightForge ?

A computer with Internet access with Internet Explorer, Chrome, Opera, or Firefox.

Do I need to install a software on my computer to be able to use FreightForge ?

No, FreightForge is a 100% online application. You access FreightForge via the Internet.

How can I start sending/receiving requests for quote?

All you need to do is sign up. After having completed the check of your company, FreightForge gives you full access to the application.

How long does it take to sign up?

For shippers it takes less than a minute. For carriers it takes about 10 minutes.

Why do I have to submit my insurance policy and my transport operator license?

FreightForge aims to connect shippers with reliable carriers. Doing business with a new partner might carry risks and FreightForge helps reduce these risks by letting shippers consult the valid insurance policy and transport operator’s license of the carriers.

Why do I need a credit card?

A credit card is the quickest and safest way to top up your credit. Accepted cards are: Visa, Visa Electron, MasterCard, Maestro.

How can I contact FreightForge if I have difficulties signing up?

Send us an email to contact@freightforge.com.

Pricing and payment

I am a shipper. How much does it cost?

Shippers use FreightForge completely free. No hidden fees whatsoever.

I am a carrier. How much does it cost?

FreightForge earns money by taking a small percentage of the price of transport quoted and the business won. (0.05% of the quoted price and 0.5% of the business won)

What are the payment options?

FreightForge’s services are prepaid. You can top up your credit by using your credit card (Visa, Visa Electron, MasterCard, Maestro).

How does FreightForge handle invoicing?

Invoices can be downloaded in signed pdf format.

Will transport payment happen via the FreightForge platform?

No. Invoicing and payment for the transport services will take place through the normal contractual process agreed by the partners.

Privacy and security

How does FreightForge protect my privacy?

Please consult our Privacy Policy.

How does FreightForge address the security issues?

FreightForge knows that security is of outmost importance, that is why we run different checks as well as regularly monitor all accounts. Our application is secured and your credentials are invisible to us. FreightForge also allows its users to report any suspicious activity. Every report submitted prompts an investigation and can eventually lead to the suspension of the account concerned.

Will shippers/carriers see my contact details?

FreightForge knows that spams and unwanted phone calls are not welcome, that is why we protect your contact details (whether you are a shipper or a carrier) throughout the process of quoting. The carrier will find the shipper’s contact details only when a quote is won.

What are cookies and do I need them enabled to use the site?

No. Cookies don’t need to be enabled to use FreightForge .

What data does FreightForge store about its users?

Please consult our Privacy Policy.

Will I receive spam if I register?

Our anti-spam policy is very strict; we never resell or communicate in any way the contact details of our accounts. Still if you want to be removed from our database please contact FreightForge directly: contact@freightforge.com
