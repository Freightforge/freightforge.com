Shipper

Spot procurement efficiency

Go beyond the simple board posting and rely on FreightForge’s advanced artificial intelligence to identify, contact and request quote submission from the carriers that actually have the capability to freight your goods. Let the work happen behind the scene and simply come back later to select your best option.

Security

Transport authorization and insurance documents for every carrier on FreightForge are available online for you to review.

Sourcing compliance

You want control on who you deal with. Create closed groups, organize them as you see fit, and share them among your team to ensure compliance with procurement policies.

Control

FreightForge is a freight quote tool. You get the quotes and you choose which one is the best. Maybe you go for the cheaper or maybe the one with the best dates or the one from your trusted provider. It is all in your hand.

Carrier

Business development

Gain business development efficiency by receiving quote requests that correspond to your profile come to your desktop. Only select the ones that you want to do.

Operational efficiency

Receive all relevant information pertaining to the quote request at once. No need for additional information to quote. No lost time in phone calls, emails and faxes for a business you haven’t won yet.

Limited competition

Only carriers with capabilities strictly corresponding to the quote request are contacted to submit a bid.

Commons

Network

Access a wide international network of shippers and carriers to freight from anywhere to anywhere.

Integration

A free and simple API to connect FreightForge to your own system.

Secure connection

FreightForge access is fully crypted through SSL security protocols. You are just as secure when working through public internet accesses, such as airports, as as you are working from your own office.

Prepaid service

FreightForge is prepaid. You keep full control of what your expenses are. You can simply recharge your account by using a credit card.

Efficient support
